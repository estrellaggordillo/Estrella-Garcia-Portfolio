About me

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 8+ years of experience turning ambiguous problems into products people actually love. I've worked across fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, and cultural institutions — always obsessing over the gap between how things feel and how they work.

From founding-stage startups to enterprise teams, I bring strategic thinking to the craft and rigour to the handoff. I believe design is a team sport — I coach, I pair, I critique, and I ship.
